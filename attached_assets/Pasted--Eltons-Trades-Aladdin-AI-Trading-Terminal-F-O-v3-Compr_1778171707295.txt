## Eltons Trades: Aladdin AI Trading Terminal (F&O v3)
### Comprehensive Project Master Specification
This document merges and codifies all requirements for **Eltons Trades**, an institutional-grade, Bloomberg-style automated trading terminal. Engineered for the Indian Equities and Derivatives (F&O) markets, this system utilizes a multi-agent AI architecture to synthesize live market telemetry, news, and risk management into high-probability trade execution.
## 1. System Architecture & Real-Time Data Pipeline
To eliminate latency, the terminal bypasses standard HTTP polling in favor of an event-driven, "Tick-to-Trade" architecture.
 * **The Source (NSE/BSE):** Direct data ingestion from the National Stock Exchange and Bombay Stock Exchange.
 * **Infrastructure (Leased Lines & DMA):** Uses Direct Market Access (DMA) logic. In a production environment, this mimics the high-speed fiber-optic leased lines used by brokers to connect to Mumbai data centers.
 * **Technical Delivery (WebSockets):** A permanent, bidirectional "pipe" (TCP connection) that pushes every price tick to the UI the millisecond it arrives.
 * **Data Levels:**
   * **Level 1 (L1):** Best Bid/Ask and Last Traded Price (LTP).
   * **Level 2 (L2):** Market Depth (Top 20 buy/sell orders) for liquidity analysis.
   * **Level 3 (Tick-by-Tick):** Full order flow integration for HFT-style analysis.
 * **Data Sources:** Integrates live RSS feeds and APIs from **Moneycontrol, Economic Times (ET), Livemint, Business Standard (BS), BSE India, and World Monitor**.
## 2. The "Aladdin" Multi-Agent AI Core
The terminal operates as a virtual trading desk where specialized agents collaborate to validate trades.

| Agent | Responsibility | Logic / Parameters |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Analyst Agent** | Quantitative Engine | Scans OHLCV every 5–18s for 15+ patterns (e.g., Morning Star, Hammer). Calculates RSI, MACD, VWAP, and SuperTrend. |
| **Researcher Agent** | Fundamental (RAG) | Pulls news context from World Monitor and BSE India. Prevents trading against macro sentiment or negative earnings. |
| **Risk Agent** | Systemic Guardrail | Enforces EMA-50 trend filters. Rejects trades if price < EMA-50. Caps position size at **1.5% - 2%** of capital. |
| **Supervisor Agent** | Executive Decision | Orchestrates data into **Claude 3.5 Sonnet / Gemini 1.5 Pro**. Outputs JSON with Entry, SL, Target, and Reasoning. |

## 3. Core Features & User Interface
The UI uses a **"Hacker-Trader" aesthetic**: dark-mode glassmorphism with neon-glow indicators and a cyber-grid background.
### **Interactive Dashboards**
 * **Live Market News Feed:** Updated every second; filtered exclusively for Indian stock market and option data.
 * **Advanced Charting:** Embedded **TradingView** widget with RSI, MACD, and Bollinger Bands. Integrated with GoCharting (order flow) and Nifty Trader.
 * **Option Chain Explorer:** Real-time matrix of LTP, Open Interest (OI) bars, and Greeks (Delta, Gamma, Theta, Vega).
   * *Black-Scholes Fallback:* If APIs lag, the terminal calculates Greeks locally on the client side.
 * **Market Overview & Institutional Flow:** Real-time FII/DII data and intraday ranges (VWAP, 52-week High/Low).
 * **Predicted Range:** AI-driven prediction of daily High/Low for all indices with detailed "Why" analysis.
### **Trading Controls**
 * **Ticker Strip:** Real-time prices for major global and domestic symbols (Nifty, Sensex, AAPL, TSLA, etc.).
 * **Trade Signals:** 3 Categories (All, F&O, Stocks) with dropdown filters (Buy only, Sell only, All).
 * **Automation:** **AUTO ON/OFF** toggle for paper trading.
 * **The Kill Switch:** An immutable safety mechanism that halts all trading if daily drawdown hits **5%**.
